# Rules
Each player logs in with their Username and Password and they get shown their target.
Once they eliminate their target, they take that person's target and that person is out.

The round carries on until there is 1 person left standing and everyone else has been eliminated.

There is a leaderboard tracking who has the most amount of eliminations.

Everybody has to be honest and only say that they have eliminated their target when they actually have.

Everything will be automated and random so nobody can cheat and so everyone can play without there needing to be an admin
